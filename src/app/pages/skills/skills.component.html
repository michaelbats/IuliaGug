Core Skills
These are my daily bread and butter with which I've been through all stages of the Dunning-Kruger Effect.
Growing Skills
These are the things i'm making a major effort towards learnning and which I'm constantly looking for opportunities to use in production.
Paused Skills
These are skills that I have at some point used in production or experimented with but for various reasons no longer actively use or learn.
